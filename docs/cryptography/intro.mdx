# Cryptography


## What is Cryptography?

Cryptography is what allows us to do all our private things, <u>privately</u>.
In CTFs, it's mostly about breaking encryption schemes, hashing algorithms, and weird encoding methods 
to find that hidden message ( most likely the flag ).

In this section, we will explore the very basics of cryptography (*encodings*) and then dive deeper into more complex concepts such as **Symmetric & Asymmetric Encryption**.
